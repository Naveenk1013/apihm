First Year

Semester 1 & 2 — Foundations
Introduction to Hospitality & Tourism Industry
Fundamentals of Rooms Division Management I & II
Fundamentals of Food & Beverage Management I & II
Business Communication & Computer Application
Fundamentals of Travel & Tourism
Indian Knowledge System & Personality Skills
Focus: Industry orientation, communication skills, digital literacy & sustainability.

Second Year

Semester 3 & 4 — Skill Advancement
Intermediate Food Production & F&B Service
Advanced Rooms Division Management
Hospitality French & Basic Hotel Accounting
Bakery, Confectionery & Bar Beverages
Food Safety, Hygiene & F&B Control
Hospitality & Tourism Law
Focus: Specialization, financial literacy, legal knowledge & hygiene standards.
3

Third Year

Semester 5 & 6 — Management
International Cuisines & F&B Operations
Front Office Administration & Management
Human Resource & Marketing Management
Hotel Engineering & Maintenance
Resort Designing, Development & Management
Event & Disaster Management
Focus: Leadership, operations management & resort & event expertise.

Fourth Year

Semester 7 & 8 — Specialization
F&B Management & Control II
Industrial Training II — Specialization (4 Months)
Eco Tourism, Ecotels & Sustainable Development
Architecture & Interior Designing
Research Project
Room Division Management II
Focus: Industry specialization, sustainability & research leadership.
