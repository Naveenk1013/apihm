## 7. BHMCT Curriculum (Accordion)
- **Label**: BHMCT Curriculum
- **Title**: A Structured 4-Year *Learning Journey*
- **Intro**: Our revised curriculum framework blends hospitality fundamentals with advanced operational skills, international exposure, and specialization — aligned to AICTE and GTU standards for global industry readiness.
- **Pills**: Food Production, Front Office, Housekeeping, F&B Management, Hospitality French, Hotel Accounting, Tourism Law, Event Management, Eco Tourism, Research Project.
- **Year 1 (Foundations)**:
  - **Subjects**: Introduction to Hospitality & Tourism; Fundamentals of Rooms Division; Fundamentals of F&B; Business Communication; Travel & Tourism; Indian Knowledge System.
  - **Focus**: Industry orientation, communication skills, digital literacy & sustainability.
- **Year 2 (Skill Advancement)**:
  - **Subjects**: Intermediate Food Production; Advanced Rooms Division; Hospitality French & Hotel Accounting; Bakery & Bar; Food Safety & F&B Control; Hospitality Law.
  - **Focus**: Specialization, financial literacy, legal knowledge & hygiene standards.
- **Year 3 (Management)**:
  - **Subjects**: International Cuisines; Front Office Administration; HR & Marketing Management; Hotel Engineering; Resort Designing; Event & Disaster Management.
  - **Focus**: Leadership, operations management & resort & event expertise.
- **Year 4 (Specialization)**:
  - **Subjects**: F&B Management II; Industrial Training II; Eco Tourism & Sustainability; Architecture & Interior Designing; Research Project; Rooms Division II.
  - **Focus**: Industry specialization, sustainability & research leadership.
